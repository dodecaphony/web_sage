Spelling correction

On this page, you can use the text correction feature. To use it, you need to select a model that will correct your text. The text can be one sentence or multiple sentences. You can enter the text in a special field or upload a file in the allowed format. The last step is optional. If you want us to collect the corrected results for further analysis, please click the checkbox. If you don't want to share your data, you can skip this step.
